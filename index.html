Research

I have experience delivering academic literature reviews to guide informed, research-based development of new technologies. A social scientist by background, I am able to combine critical thinking with a range of qualitative and quantitative research methods to deliver focussed interdisciplinary research.

Design

I take a unique approach to user research and design, focussing on users' relationships to principles of technology ethics. I apply approaches such as critical pedagogy and participatory action research to enable open discourse between designers and users. This allows me to focus on the why of UX Design, not just the how.

Digital Ethics

I have worked with clients from across the public and private sectors to help them understand, address and react to the ethical risks and opportunities of technology. Using techniques such as workshops, cultural analysis and user research, I work with clients to co-deliver realistic and practical digital ethics strategies.
